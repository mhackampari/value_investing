# Investment Research

A personal repository for analyzing businesses and companies under a critical, fact-based lens — grounded in the value investing tradition of Graham, Buffett, and Munger.

## Purpose

Each analysis aims to answer one question: **Is this a wonderful business at the right price?**

That means cutting through narrative and hype to focus on what actually matters — durable competitive advantages, capital allocation quality, financial track record, and whether the current price offers a margin of safety relative to intrinsic value.

## Approach

- Facts and numbers first. Opinions are labeled as such.
- Red flags are called out explicitly, even when the broader view is positive.
- Verdicts are clear: **Buy / Watch / Pass** — with the key condition that would change the view.
